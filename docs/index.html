What Has Been Built

Not a prototype. A production-grade monorepo with real data models, seed pipelines, and operational surfaces.

Multilingual Guest OS

10-language PWA with intent-based concierge, venue map, cultural food discovery, emergency mode, rewards, and demo sessions for 6 visitor personas.

Agentic Concierge + RAG

Intent parser, tool router, cultural ranker, pgvector retrieval, and sponsor injection engine with trace metadata on every response.

Deterministic Emergency System

8 emergency phrases with approved translations across all languages. Safety logic always outranks sponsor logic. No hallucination allowed.

3-Tier Sponsor Engine

Smart Placement, Cultural Concierge, and Reward Engine tiers. 12 seeded campaigns with impression, click, and conversion attribution.

Revenue Console

Campaign performance, language breakdown, intent distribution, zone heat maps, and real-time revenue simulation with CFR and ROI analytics.

Full Stack Infrastructure
