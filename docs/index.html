The System

What's Been Built

A production-grade monorepo with real data, real cultural profiles, and real sponsor campaigns. Not a mockup.

🤖

AI Concierge + RAG

15 MCP tools, pgvector retrieval, intent classification, cultural ranking, and sponsor injection — with trace metadata on every response.

🌍

10-Language Fan OS

Multilingual PWA with real-time intent detection, cultural food discovery, venue wayfinding, rewards, and emergency safety mode.

💰

3-Tier Sponsor Engine

Smart Placement (CPM), Cultural Concierge (CPC), and Reward Engine (Rev Share). 12 seeded campaigns with full attribution.

🚨

Deterministic Emergency

8 approved phrases across all languages. Safety always outranks sponsor logic. No hallucination. No override.

📞

Telecom Pipeline

Telnyx SMS/Voice integration. Inbound message processing, language detection, outbound delivery, and session tracking.

🏗️

Production Stack

Next.js 15 · NestJS · Prisma 6 · PostgreSQL 16 + pgvector · Redis 7 · BullMQ · Turborepo · Docker Compose.
