# Product Overview — Wilkins Media Global Guest OS

## What It Is

A production-grade, white-label multilingual event platform that helps international visitors:

- Navigate events in their own language
- Discover culturally relevant food and services
- Access emergency help with deterministic, approved translations
- Communicate with staff through a real-time translation bridge
- Find nearby city services (hospitals, transit, ATMs, pharmacies)

## Who It's For

- **International event guests** attending FIFA, expos, concerts, conventions
- **Event operators** managing multilingual venues and staff
- **Sponsors** looking to reach international audiences with relevant promotions
- **City services** providing contextual support around events

## Differentiators

1. **Multilingual navigation** — not just translated text, but language-aware map POIs and search
2. **Cultural personalization** — recommendations ranked by dietary preferences, transport habits, and cultural behavior
3. **Emergency usability** — deterministic approved phrase packs (not AI-generated), full-screen mode, voice playback
4. **Sponsor monetization** — transparent, culturally-targeted injection that never degrades trust
5. **White-label deployment** — tenant-level branding for any event worldwide

## Target Events

- FIFA World Cup 2026 (Atlanta host city — first deployment)
- Stadium events and sports championships
- International expos and trade shows
- Concerts and festivals
- Hospitality activations and conventions

## Positioning

> Wilkins Media Global Guest OS helps international visitors navigate events, discover relevant local services, communicate instantly in their own language, and access support in real time.
