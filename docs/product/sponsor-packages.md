# Tap FIFA — Sponsor Packages

## Package A — Route Sponsor
Positioning:
Own a high-intent route surface (Directions, Food, Rewards, Help, or VIP).

Includes:
- Featured sponsor card in selected route flow
- CTA with click tracking
- Language targeting
- Basic performance summary

Best for:
- Local and regional brands testing event demand.

## Package B — Intent Sponsor
Positioning:
Match sponsor placement to guest intent signals in real time.

Includes:
- Intent-triggered placements (example: food intent -> dining offer)
- Route + language targeting
- Offer/reward object support
- Mid-event creative swap window
- Enhanced analytics

Best for:
- National brands optimizing conversion quality.

## Package C — Reward Sponsor
Positioning:
Tie sponsor value to measurable guest actions and redemptions.

Includes:
- Sponsor-linked reward challenge
- Coupon/badge integration
- Redemption tracking
- Post-event attribution report

Best for:
- Brands focused on direct response and measurable activation.

## Package D — VIP Signature Sponsor
Positioning:
Premium presence across VIP and high-value fan journeys.

Includes:
- Priority placement in VIP route
- Premium creative treatment
- Hospitality-friendly call-to-action flows
- Executive reporting

Best for:
- Premium and luxury partners.

## Add-Ons
- Multi-language creative adaptation
- Geo/time-window optimization
- A/B creative tests
- Live optimization support desk

## Packaging Notes
- Keep sponsored surfaces clearly labeled.
- Preserve utility-first UX (no intrusive interruptions).
- Measure success by useful outcomes, not only raw impressions.
