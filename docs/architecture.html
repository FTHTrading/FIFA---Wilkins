Architecture

Flow

Five-Step Request Flow

1Fan sends a request (web, text, or voice).
2System detects language and what the fan needs.
3Platform finds the best matching info from trusted data.
4If appropriate, it adds a sponsor offer.
5Response is delivered and results are tracked.
